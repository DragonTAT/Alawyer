# SeedKB Notice

## Purpose

The files under `SeedKB/` are sample knowledge-base materials for development,
local testing, and demonstration of retrieval and citation behavior.

## Source and Licensing Statement

- The content is maintained by this project's contributors.
- It is intended as a practical summary/reference material for the demo flow.
- Any references to laws or regulations should be independently verified against
official and latest published legal texts.

## Usage Limits

- Do not treat these files as authoritative legal sources.
- Do not use generated outputs as formal legal advice.
